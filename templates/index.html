Our Story

YouAdMe, derives from the word You Advertise Me. "You", the consumer, helps "Me", the business owner to advertise the business’s products and services from your own experience.

YouAdMe Social E-Commerce application started with the intention of helping businesses to grow from better understanding their consumers’ thoughts and needs through their reviews, at the same time help drive attention towards the business via peer recommendations. Consumers are given the opportunity to show love to the business they support while spreading the goodness within their circle and help the growth of business revenue.
